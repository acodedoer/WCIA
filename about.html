Brief History

The school was established on 10th May 2013, and commenced operation in the year 2014 for the 2014/2015 academic session with just 5 pupils and seven staf, including the school's first head teacher, Mr. AbdulMalik Yusuf Babatunde. By the end of the 2014/2015 academic year, the school has enrolled the number of 39 pupils and also increased in number of staff.

The school is owned by the parent company - White Crescent Educational Services.

Our Mission

Nurturing our Children into young men and women who are academically Competitive and morally sound by inculcating a positive Muslim Identity and devotion to God through the teachings of Islam and a strong Commitment to Family, Community and Humanity.

Our Vision

To become an Ultra-Modern Citadel where Muslim Children receive Quality Education plus an Ideal Islamic Upbringing.
